About Us

The school was started on 10.6.1996 as per the minutes of the meeting of the Managing committee of the A.P. Police Welfare Society held on 23.5.1996 in the chamber of D.G.P., A.P., Hyderabad and recognized vide Rc.No.11/A/97, D.E.O., E.G. Dist. of Andhra Pradesh from Nursery to 10thclass. 61 teaching staff and 09 non-teaching staff are working in the School. 1809 students are studying in the School.

Many parents are inclined to admit their children in our institution, which offers a difference system of all round development and positive personality of the children who are adopting a special of system of important skills in managing their personality in this society, by introducing extracurricular activities like dance, Music and Martial Arts. Special coaching to slow learners is being adopted regularly. And these the factors resulted in the pouring of the admissions in to our institutions.

Our Features

Accommodation

54 rooms are available in the school, student’s desks available from LKG to 10th Standard students

Library

A separate room with seating is provided for library. The number of books including reference books is 2004

Laboratory

There is laboratory with the required furniture. Chemicals and all items of apparatus are also available

Sports

Games materials are provided by the management and children play in both the morning and evening

Computer Lab

Separate room was provided for computer education. 50 computers are available

Digital Class Room

Digital equipment was installed in 6 class rooms for digital teaching
